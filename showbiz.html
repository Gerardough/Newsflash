NIKI to return to PH for 'Buzz' concert tour

By Josiah Antonio, ABS-CBN News Published May 14, 2024 09:37 PM PHT

  1. NIKI to return to PH for 'Buzz' concert tour
  2. Ex-Sponge Cola drummer to drop song dedicated to TWICE

MANILA — Indonesian singer NIKI is set to return to the Philippines next year for a 2-day concert.

In an announcement, NIKI said that she will be holding her "Buzz" concert tour on February 11 and 12, 2025. "BUZZ WORLD TOUR 2024-2025 !! With support from two of my favorite songwriters @allisonponthier & @AmyAllenMusic," the Indonesian singer said in the caption. NIKI is set to release her third studio album "Buzz" on August 9 and will also be touring around the world. She released the album's new single "Too Much Of A Good Thing" this month to kick off the new era. NIKI showcased her latest album "Nicole" in at her first solo concert in the Philippines, held at the Mall of Asia Arena last September 2023. NIKI showcases 'Nicole' at sold-out concert in PH "It's so good to be back in Manila. It's like my first ever headline arena show ever in my career, you guys sold out in like minutes. Thank you so, so much. This would be nothing without you. Thank you for being here," she said in the concert. NIKI is known for her songs like "lowkey", "Every Summertime", "Backburner", among others.
