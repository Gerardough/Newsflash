BINI reaches 5 million monthly listeners on Spotify

By Josiah Antonio, ABS-CBN News
Published May 14, 2024 10:07 AM PHT

  1. NIKI to return to PH for 'Buzz' concert tour
  2. Ex-Sponge Cola drummer to drop song dedicated to TWICE
  3. BINI reaches 5 million monthly listeners on Spotify

MANILA — OPM girl group BINI has garnered 5 million monthly listeners on the online streaming platform Spotify, Star Music confirmed over the weekend.

The rise of streams on Spotify follows the virality of their songs "Pantropiko" and "Salamin, Salamin" early this year. Composed of Mikha, Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Jhoanna and Sheena, BINI has steadily risen in popularity in the past months. They made their debut in 2021 with the single "Born to Win," and are also known for singles such as "Na, Na, Na," "Huwag Muna Tayong Umuwi," "Lagi," among others. Last March, BINI released their extended play "Talaarawan" which featured the viral tracks "Pantropiko" and "Salamin, Salamin." They will hold their first solo concert, "BINIverse," on June 28, 29, and 30 at the New Frontier Theater in Quezon City.
