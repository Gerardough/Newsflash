Moderate to heavy rains seen over Metro Manila, seven provinces

By GMA Integrated News
Published May 14, 2024 1:24pm Updated May 14, 2024 3:59pm

  1. Moderate to heavy rains seen over Metro Manila
  2. PH transitioning to rainy season - PAGASA
  3. Cloudy skies, rain showers to prevail across PH

A thunderstorm advisory was raised over Metro Manila and nearby provinces on Tuesday afternoon, giving respite from the extreme heat, the state weather bureau PAGASA said.

In an advisory issued at 2:18 p.m., moderate to heavy rains with lightning and strong winds are expected over the capital region, Nueva Ecijia, Tarlac, Bataan, Batangas, Rizal, Laguna, and Quezon Province. Meanwhile, heavy to intense rains along with lightning and strong winds are being experienced in Zambales (Santa Cruz, Candelaria, Masinloc, Palauig, San Marcelino, Botolan), Cavite (Trece Martires,Tanza, Naic, General Trias), Bulacan (Pulilan, Calumpit), and Pampanga (Apalit) and may persist for up to two hours.
